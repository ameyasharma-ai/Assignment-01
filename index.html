Fun Facts & Trivia

Did you know?

First Computer Programmer

Ada Lovelace is considered to be the first computer programmer. She realized that the computer could follow a series of simple instructions, a program, to perform a complex calculation.

The first computer mouse

The first computer mouse was made of wood! It was invented by Doug Engelbart in 1964.
